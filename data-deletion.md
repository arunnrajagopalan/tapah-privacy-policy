# Data Deletion - Tapah

## How to Delete Your Data

### In-App Deletion (Recommended)

1. Open the Tapah app
2. Go to the **Profile** tab (bottom navigation)
3. Scroll down to the **Account** section
4. Tap **Delete Account**
5. Type **DELETE** to confirm
6. All your data will be permanently removed

### What Gets Deleted

- Your account and profile information
- All daily logs, symptom records, and food test results
- Program history and progress data
- All preferences and settings

### Data Stored with Google Services

Tapah uses Firebase Analytics and Crashlytics. Aggregated analytics data retained by Google is not personally identifiable and cannot be deleted on a per-user basis. For more information, see [Google's data retention policies](https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy).
